Biography

Zechao Li is currently a Professor and vice dean of the School of Computer Science and Engeering, the School of Artificial Intelligence, and the School of Software Engeering at the Nanjing University of Science and Technology. He received the Bachelor’s degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2008 and the Doctor degree from National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2013. He has authored over 90 papers in top-tier journals and conferences. His research interests include multimedia analysis, object detection, semantic segmentation, few-shot learning, etc. He was a recipient of the Best Paper Award in ACM Multimedia Asia 2020, and the Best Student Paper Award in ICIMCS 2018. He has served as an Associate Editor for IEEE TPAMI, TNNLS, etc.

Research Interests
