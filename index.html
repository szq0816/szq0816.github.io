Biography

I am a full Professor at the Faculty of Information Engineering and Automation, Kunming University of Science and Technology, China. I received the Ph.D. degree from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, China. Previously, I was a visiting scholar with the School of Information and Communication Technology, Griffith University, QLD, Australia, from November 2014 to May 2015, under the supervision of Professor Jun Zhou. Before joining Kunming University of Science and Technology, I worked as a postdoctoral at Jiangnan University for four years, working under the guidance of Professor Xiao-Jun Wu.

Research Interests

Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Bioinformatics, Pattern Recognition.
