Biography

He is a full Professor at the Faculty of Information Engineering and Automation, Kunming University of Science and Technology, China. He received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, China. Previously, he was a visiting scholar at the School of Information and Communication Technology, Griffith University, QLD, Australia, from November 2014 to May 2015, under the supervision of Professor Jun Zhou. Before joining Kunming University of Science and Technology, He worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Jiangnan University for four years under the guidance of Professor Xiao-Jun Wu.

Research Interests

Multi-view clustering, cross-modal retrieval, scRNA-seq clustering, hyperspectral image processing, fault diagnosis.
